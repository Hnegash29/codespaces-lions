All About Lions!



Facts about Lions

Lions are big cats native to the Saharan region of Africa aand India. Their roars can be heard up to 5 miles away. They are known for being the most laziest big cats, sleeping up to 20 hours a day! Isn't that interesting? As male lions age, there manes get darker. This is a sign of a healty lion. Female lions tend to live from 15-16 years and male lions tend to 8-10 years. They are carnivorous which means that they are meat eaters.

Lion Species

There is only one specie of Lion but there are two sub species of lions. The Panthera leo Lion and the Panthera Leo melanochaita. The panthera leo leo are found in regions such as, North Africa, West Africa, Central Africa, and Asia. The panthera leo melanochaita are mainly in regions like East and Southern Africa.
